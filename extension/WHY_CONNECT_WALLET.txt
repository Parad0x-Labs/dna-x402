🤔 WHY DOES PDX DARK ASK TO CONNECT A WALLET?

PDX Dark is NOT a wallet replacement - it's a PRIVACY LAYER!

Think of it like this:
─────────────────────────
🔐 SAFE (Your Wallet)     = Your money lives here
🕵️  PDX Dark (Privacy)    = Adds zero-knowledge privacy
🔗 CONNECTION            = Links them together

PDX Dark works WITH your existing wallet:
• Phantom ✅
• Solflare ✅
• Backpack ✅
• Any Solana wallet ✅

What happens when you connect:
1. PDX can see your balance (to show amounts)
2. PDX can request signatures (to send private transfers)
3. Your funds stay 100% in your wallet
4. PDX adds privacy using zero-knowledge proofs

This is the same as how Uniswap connects to MetaMask,
or how dApps connect to wallets - it's normal and safe!

Your wallet = Your money
PDX Dark = Privacy protection

Together = Anonymous transfers! 🛡️🔒🕵️
